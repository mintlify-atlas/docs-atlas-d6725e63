---

When you run `generate_showcase.py`, the script composites your exported logo PNG against one of 12 curated background styles and renders a 16:9, 2K showcase image via the Gemini image generation API. Each style has a specific visual language and is suited to particular product categories. This page documents all 12 styles so you can choose deliberately rather than guessing.

<Note>
Dark styles render your logo in **pure white (#FFFFFF)**. Light styles render your logo in **pure black (#000000)**. The script applies this automatically based on the style you select — you do not need to prepare separate logo files.
</Note>

---

| Key | Display name | Mode | Saturation | Best for |

## Dark styles

Dark styles use a predominantly dark background. Your logo is rendered in pure white for maximum contrast.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="void — The Void (绝对虚空)">

**Concept**: Absolute minimalism and mystery.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Pure black (#000000)
- Noise: Extremely fine silver/white high-contrast micro noise
- Texture: Cold, sharp electronic film grain
- Atmosphere: Minimal — only a faint icy white or blue glow at the extreme corner, like distant starlight at the edge of the universe
- Mood: Infinite void, cosmic distance, total restraint

**Suitable for**: Hardcore tech, data security, infrastructure products.

**Saturation**: Extremely low (5%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
THE VOID (绝对虚空)
Absolute black (#000000) background with extremely fine silver/white high-contrast micro noise.
Cold, sharp electronic film grain texture. Minimal atmosphere light - only a faint, icy white or blue glow
at the extreme corner, like distant starlight at the edge of the universe.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="frosted — Frosted Horizon (磨砂穹顶)">

**Concept**: Modern breathing space with physical thickness.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Deep titanium gray or midnight slate gray — not pure black
- Noise: Organic film-like dust texture
- Texture: Unpolished rough metal or stone surface
- Atmosphere: Large area low-saturation cold gray-blue light halo, edges completely dissolved like mist
- Mood: Sophisticated, breathable, Apple-like presentation quality

**Suitable for**: Premium products, design-focused brands.

**Saturation**: Low (10%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
FROSTED HORIZON (磨砂穹顶)
Deep titanium gray or midnight slate gray base, not pure black. Organic film-like dust noise texture,
resembling unpolished rough metal or stone surface. Large area but extremely low saturation cold-toned
light halo (low-saturation gray-blue), edges completely dissolved like mist.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="fluid — Fluid Abyss (流体深渊)">

**Concept**: AI-native with data fluidity.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Deep midnight purple or extremely dark Klein blue
- Noise: Slight color-tinted noise blending with the base
- Texture: Deep-sea sediment or nebula quality
- Atmosphere: Fluid fusion — dark orange on the right, dark blue on the left, slowly interweaving in the dark space center
- Mood: Mysterious, dynamic, computational

**Suitable for**: AI products, dynamic systems, data visualization.

**Saturation**: Medium (20%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
FLUID ABYSS (流体深渊)
Deep midnight purple or extremely dark Klein blue base. Noise texture with slight color tint,
blending with the base to create deep-sea sediment or nebula texture. Fluid fusion light -
dark orange on right side, dark blue on left side, slowly interweaving in the dark space center.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="spotlight — Studio Spotlight (物理影棚)">

**Concept**: Physical studio lighting simulation.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Extremely dark warm carbon gray
- Noise: Slightly larger grain simulating low-light camera photography
- Texture: Paper print grain in weak light
- Atmosphere: Single-side softbox or spotlight creating a natural vignette
- Mood: Editorial magazine quality, professional photography feel

**Suitable for**: Editorial design, magazine-style brand presentations.

**Saturation**: None (0%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
STUDIO SPOTLIGHT (物理影棚)
Extremely dark warm carbon gray base. Slightly larger grain simulating low-light camera photography,
like paper print grain in weak light. Single-side softbox or spotlight creating natural vignette,
editorial magazine quality with professional photography feel.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="analog_liquid — Analog Liquid (物理流体)">

**Concept**: Physical fluid textures with conflict aesthetics.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Solid color — exactly one of: vibrant orange (#FF6B00), Klein blue (#002FA7), or lime green (#00FF41)
- Noise: Microscopic cellular patterns or extreme grainy "thermal imaging" roughness
- Texture: Metallic shimmer overlay — gold dust flow, metallic mica powder, iridescent pigments creating rainbow oil slick effects; dry mineral textures like crushed gemstones; macro photography of copper oxidation, rust patterns, gold leaf fragments
- Atmosphere: Extreme contrast between chaotic organic texture and ultra-clean sharp vector logo
- Mood: Chemical reaction, artistic creativity, inspiration

**Suitable for**: Creative tools, artistic brands, experimental products.

**Saturation**: High (40–60%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
ANALOG LIQUID (物理流体)
Solid color base - choose ONE color only: vibrant orange (#FF6B00), Klein blue (#002FA7), or lime green (#00FF41).
Physical liquid textures with metallic shimmer overlaying the solid base - gold dust flow, metallic mica powder
suspended in liquid, iridescent pigments creating rainbow oil slick effects. Dry mineral textures like crushed
gemstones. Macro photography of natural materials - copper oxidation, rust patterns, gold leaf fragments.
Extreme grainy texture like thermal imaging or pushed film grain. Create maximum contrast between chaotic
organic texture and ultra-clean sharp vector logo.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="led_matrix — LED Matrix (数字硬件)">

**Concept**: Digital retro and pixel matrix.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Black background with glowing dot matrix patterns
- Noise: CRT display artifacts, halftone printing dots
- Texture: Old-school LED billboard, retro computer displays
- Atmosphere: Waves of glowing points creating depth; the logo floats as a solid entity above the matrix
- Mood: Hardcore geek, cyberpunk, retro-futurism

**Suitable for**: AI computing, Web3, data services, electronic hardware.

**Saturation**: Medium (25–35%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
LED MATRIX (数字硬件)
Black background with glowing dot matrix patterns creating waves of light. Simulate old-school CRT displays,
LED billboards, or halftone printing dots. Retro computer display artifacts with modern execution.
Waves of glowing points creating depth, logo as solid entity floating above. Cyberpunk and retro-futurism
aesthetics with hardcore geek appeal.
```

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Light styles

Light styles use a predominantly light background. Your logo is rendered in pure black for maximum contrast.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="editorial — Editorial Paper (纸本编辑)">

**Concept**: High-end specialty paper with extreme whitespace.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Off-white, alabaster, or pearl white — not pure white
- Noise: High-grade watercolor or rough art paper texture
- Texture: Physical paper tactile quality suggestion
- Atmosphere: Natural light diffuse reflection, slight warm gray vignette in corners
- Mood: Humanistic, independent magazine aesthetic

**Suitable for**: Humanistic brands, serious professional services.

**Saturation**: Very low (5%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
EDITORIAL PAPER (纸本编辑)
Off-white, alabaster, or pearl white base (not pure white). High-grade watercolor or rough art paper
texture suggesting physical paper tactile quality. Natural light diffuse reflection with slight warm
gray vignette in corners. Humanistic, independent magazine aesthetic.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="iridescent — Iridescent Frost (幻彩透砂)">

**Concept**: Minimal tech with optical material beauty.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Extremely light silver-gray or cold white
- Noise: Extremely fine micro noise simulating high-density frosted glass or sandblasted aluminum
- Texture: Sandblasted aluminum surface quality
- Atmosphere: Soft holographic/iridescent colors — faint light purple, light blue, or soft pink fluid diffused through thick frosted glass
- Mood: Apple hardware render quality, lightweight, optical

**Suitable for**: Tech products, hardware, scientific applications.

**Saturation**: Medium (25%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
IRIDESCENT FROST (幻彩透砂)
Extremely light silver-gray or cold white base, creating calm, rational experimental space.
Extremely fine micro noise, simulating high-density frosted glass or sandblasted aluminum surface.
Restrained holographic/iridescent atmosphere light - faint low-saturation light purple, light blue
or soft pink fluid diffused light in the clean background depth, like through thick frosted glass.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="morning — Morning Aura (晨雾光域)">

**Concept**: AI softness with approachability.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Warm ivory or extremely light cream color
- Noise: Soft noise blending into the base like morning mist or dust
- Texture: Thin layer of atmospheric haze
- Atmosphere: Large area blurred low-saturation pastel colors (mint green, baby blue, dawn orange) dissolving into warm white
- Mood: Warm, intelligent, pressure-free

**Suitable for**: User-friendly AI products, accessible consumer products.

**Saturation**: Low (15%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
MORNING AURA (晨雾光域)
Warm ivory or extremely light cream color base. Soft noise blending into base like morning mist or dust,
creating thin layer of atmospheric haze. Large area blurred low-saturation pastel colors (mint green,
baby blue, dawn orange) dissolving into warm white. Warm, intelligent, pressure-free atmosphere.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="clinical — Clinical Studio (无菌影棚)">

**Concept**: Spatial order with high contrast.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Pure white or extremely light cold gray
- Noise: High-frequency sharp cold-toned digital micro noise with enhanced sharpness
- Texture: Enhanced sharpness, sterile space quality
- Atmosphere: Pure light/shadow structure — large softbox from top or side creating a smooth gray-white gradient
- Mood: Sterile space, geometric order, 3D depth in a 2D presentation

**Suitable for**: Algorithm-driven, data-centric, confident brands.

**Saturation**: Minimal (2%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
CLINICAL STUDIO (无菌影棚)
Pure white or extremely light cold gray base. High-frequency sharp cold-toned digital micro noise with
enhanced sharpness. Pure light/shadow structure - large softbox from top/side creating smooth gray-white
gradient. Sterile space with geometric order, creating 3D depth in 2D presentation.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ui_container — UI Container (容器化界面)">

**Concept**: Digital product native feel with micro-depth.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: Clean gradient or solid color background
- Noise: Minimal digital noise
- Texture: Frosted glass container effect with rounded corners and subtle transparency — similar to an app icon base
- Atmosphere: UI-native presentation with micro-shadows creating depth illusion
- Mood: Interactive, product-ready, digital asset quality

**Suitable for**: Digital products, apps, SaaS platforms, UI/UX-focused brands.

**Saturation**: Low to medium (10–20%).

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
UI CONTAINER (容器化界面)
Clean gradient or solid color background with minimal digital noise. Frosted glass container effect
(like app icon base) with rounded corners and subtle transparency. Micro-shadows creating depth illusion.
UI-native presentation suggesting interactivity and digital product context. Logo sits in transparent
container with modern interface design language.
```

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="swiss_flat — Swiss Flat (瑞士扁平)">

**Concept**: Absolute flatness and timeless authority.

**Visual characteristics**:
- Base: 100% pure solid color — deep vintage green, rich burgundy, or classic navy
- Noise: None — absolute flatness
- Texture: None — pure graphic design, zero tricks
- Atmosphere: Zero gradients, zero effects — just perfect color and form
- Mood: Extreme confidence, classic authority, environmental and sustainability associations

**Suitable for**: Established brands, environmental or sustainable products, classic institutions.

**Saturation**: High, but sophisticated — rich, deep colors only.

**Full prompt description used by the script**:

```
SWISS FLAT (瑞士扁平)
100% pure solid color background - deep vintage green, rich burgundy, or classic navy. Absolutely no
gradients, no noise, no effects. Pure graphic design with zero tricks. Just perfect color and form.
Extreme confidence and timeless authority. Classic Swiss International Style with absolute flatness.
```

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
